    PDF compatibility tips
    • Prefer the original (non-scanned) PDF from your bank.
    • We detect dates like 01 Oct 2025, 01/10/2025, or 2025-10-01.
    • Amounts in Debit/Credit columns or a single Amount column (CR means income).
    • Continuation lines are merged into the nearest transaction description.
